Magic Canvas

Welcome to Hogwarts!
Build your own magic canvas with the tools on both sides. Start by choosing a size and background color for your canvas. You can then choose a color, a symbol and one form of animation, if you would like.
Hit the generate button and your symbol will apear on the canvas. You can also start and stop the animation by hitting the buttons underneath the canvas.
Hit the delete button to clear the entire canvas. Enter a name for your finished picture and press "save" to save it.
You can find your saved pictures on the right side.
